Note too that the toLowerCase method converts all the characters of the string to lowercase, not just the initial letters. For example, "ChEyEnNe" becomes "cheyenne." You could go the other way and convert everything to upper-case, then test against "CHEYENNE," "SANTA FE, " etc. Most coders prefer the lower-case method.
