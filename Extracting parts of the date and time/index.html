Methods Gets Range Example
getDay() days of week 0 - 6 0 is Sunday
getMonth() month 0 - 11 0 is January
getDate() day of month 1 - 13 1 is 1st of month
getFullYear() Year 2017
getHours() hour 0 - 23 0 is midnight 12, is noon, 23 is 11 p.m
getMinutes() minutes 0 - 59
getSeconds() second 0 - 59
getMilliseconds() millisecond 0.999
getTime() Milliseconds since midenight, January 1 2017

Points To Remember

  • getDate gives you anumber for the day of the month.
  • getFullYear gives you a 4-digit number for the year.
  • getHours gives you a number from 0 through 23 corresponding to midnight through 11.
  • getMinutes gives you a number from 0 through 59.
  • getSecondsgives you a number from 0 through 59.
  • getMilliseconds gives you a number from 0 through 999.
  • getTime gives you the number of milliseconds that have elapsed since midnight Aug 1, 2017
